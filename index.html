Ghana

Ghana, located in West Africa, is a country known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stable governance. It is situated along the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean, sharing borders with Côte d'Ivoire to the west, Burkina Faso to the north, and Togo to the east. Its capital city is Accra, and English serves as the official language, reflecting its colonial past.
